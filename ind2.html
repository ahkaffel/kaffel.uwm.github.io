Executive Profile

Dr. Ahmed Kaffel is an Applied Mathematician, Engineering Scientist, and Civil Engineer with internationally recognized expertise in mathematical modeling, computational science, scientific computing, and numerical simulation of complex physical, engineering, and biomedical systems. His research focuses on developing rigorous mathematical models, advanced numerical algorithms, and high-performance computational frameworks for solving multiscale and multiphysics problems arising in fluid mechanics, porous media transport, biomedical systems, medical imaging, and engineering applications. Dr. Kaffel earned his Ph.D. and M.S. in Mathematics from Virginia Polytechnic Institute and State University (Virginia Tech), and advanced engineering degrees including a Master’s in Mathematical Engineering and a National Engineering Diploma in Civil Engineering. He has held research and faculty appointments at Brown University, the University of Maryland, Prince Sultan University, Marquette University, and the University of Wisconsin–Milwaukee. His work integrates applied mathematics, computational physics, engineering science, and scientific computing to develop predictive simulation tools and computational frameworks that address fundamental scientific challenges and enable technological innovation across academic, industrial, and biomedical domains.
