Research Areas & Scientific Contributions

My research focuses on the development of rigorous mathematical models, advanced numerical methods, and high-performance computational frameworks for complex physical and engineering systems. This work integrates applied mathematics, scientific computing, fluid mechanics, porous media transport, inverse problems, and data-driven modeling to address fundamental scientific challenges and enable predictive simulation across engineering, biomedical, and environmental applications.

Primary Research Areas

Applied Mathematics & Differential Equations

  • Partial differential equations (PDEs) and dynamical systems
  • Nonlinear stability analysis and bifurcation theory
  • Mathematical modeling of physical and engineering systems
  • Analytical and asymptotic methods
  • Multiscale mathematical modeling

Scientific Computing & Numerical Methods

  • Finite Element, Finite Volume, and Finite Difference methods
  • High-order numerical schemes (ENO/WENO, spectral methods)
  • Numerical linear algebra and iterative solvers
  • Multiphysics simulation
  • Stability and convergence analysis of numerical methods

High-Performance Scientific Computing

  • Parallel algorithms and scalable computational frameworks
  • Large-scale simulation of PDE-based systems
  • GPU acceleration and distributed computing
  • Computational optimization and efficiency
  • Scientific software development

Fluid Mechanics & Hydrodynamic Stability

  • Stability and transition to turbulence
  • Viscous and viscoelastic flow dynamics
  • Interfacial instability and multiphase flow
  • Free-surface and shear flows
  • Computational fluid dynamics

Porous Media & Multiscale Transport

  • Flow and transport in porous media
  • Multiscale modeling and volume averaging
  • Unsaturated and multiphase transport
  • Coupled flow and deformation
  • Applications in engineering and biological systems

Inverse Problems & Scientific Imaging

  • Mathematical inverse problems
  • Computational imaging and reconstruction
  • Regularization and parameter estimation

  • Data-driven inverse modeling

Computational Modeling in Science & Engineering

  • Multiphysics simulation
  • Computational modeling of physical systems
  • Engineering and biomedical applications
  • Predictive simulation
  • Model validation and verification

Data-Driven Modeling & Scientific Machine Learning

  • Data-informed mathematical modeling
  • Physics-informed machine learning
  • Parameter estimation and uncertainty quantification
  • Model calibration and validation

Computational Biomedical Modeling

  • Mathematical modeling of physiological systems

  • Biomedical imaging and inverse modeling
  • Computational physiology
  • Multiscale biological systems modeling

Current Research Directions

Multiscale Modeling of Transport in Porous Media

Development of rigorous mathematical and computational frameworks to model transport phenomena in porous structures, with applications in engineering materials and biological systems.

Hydrodynamic Stability and Transition to Turbulence

Investigation of instability mechanisms and nonlinear flow evolution using analytical methods and high-fidelity numerical simulation.

High-Performance Simulation of Complex Physical Systems

Design of scalable computational methods for large-scale multiphysics simulation and predictive modeling.
